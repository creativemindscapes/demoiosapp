CAREER KARMA

Rotteinia joined Career Karma in December of 2019 while taking a vacation in Reno to figure out what her next step would be now that she had an empty nest.
Preparing for bootcamp at either Flatiron or Thinkful (previous plan) or an AMAZING apprenticeship at Pandora, Rotteinia started a small 20 women coding squad;
appropriately named "The Coding Queens Study Group."
The groups main purpose is to provide a means to share resources, stories, motivational help, study groups and challenges for women
with certain constraints to learning and/or work to help find valid solutions while finding new and interesting ways to study. ie.

  • 1. Reading a book outloud to be recorded so a busy mom can listen to her studies while the kids are up
  • 2. Taking notes from a particular reading or video to help another mom having troubles understanding.
  • 3. 24 hour access to other moms that may have gone through or be going through the same issues
  • 4. We treat each other with OHANA so no one gets left behind and everyone benefits the same
  • 5. Motivational messages throughout the week that may or may not apply to what is going on in and outside the app
  • 6. A place to call home...even when you are sitting in your home..or perhaps lack one
